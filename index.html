First Responder Challenges

First Responders (FRs) are experiencing job-related stress affecting their resiliency and performance. Seeking help is not easy and access to psychological-based resources, services, and tools is limited.

Effects of Stress & Exposure Include:
  • Stress
  • Physical Injury
  • Substance Abuse
  • PTSD
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Domestic Abuse
  • Suicide
  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Limited access to resources
Department Challenges
  • Rising number of stress-related challanges with officers
  • Stress-related issues are costly and reduce productivity
  • Stigma against psychological help
  • Officer depression and suicide rates higher than the public
  • Decline in physical fitness readiness
The Solution

mResilience is a customized program, licensed to first responder agencies to develop, maintain, and rebuild psychological resilience. It's a combation of in-person training and a companion mobile application.

Why mResilience
Program Offers
  • Capitalize on the values of officers
  • Provides access to department, local and national resources
  • Discreet and personal tools to build and support resiliency
  • Use the App is 100% confidential
  • Available 24/7
  • Stress-management and resiliency building toos
  • Goal-setting and tracking
  • Easy access to Peer Support Network and other department resources
  • Self-regulations tools to maintain personal resilience
  • Customized in-person training
Our Vision
  • First Responders can naviate difficult situations and stay resilience
  • Effective stress management and performance-enhancing tools are available 24/7
  • Department programs are reinforced through improved access
  • Our program helps reverse the trends of stress-related challenges of officers and departments
